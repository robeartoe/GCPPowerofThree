Rules:

Make sure to sign up to receive $100 in free GCP credit to use for your project, and to submit your entry.

Create a new project, or use an existing project, and integrate the Google Cloud Platform. It can be with compute engines, app engines, firebase, apis, databases, or a combination of all of them!

You can team up, or you can work on your own! There will be enough prizes for 4 people per project however, so keep that in mind when creating groups!

When you're ready to showcase your project on the website, be ready to think of a project name, explain what it does, and how it uses GCP. You can also include your Github and an image. The more the better!

Most importantly, have fun!

If you have any questions, or need help with the Google Cloud Platform, feel free to email Robert Ruiz at robert@robertjruiz.com or send a DM on ACM's slack channel!

Prizes:

Prizes will be announced soon!

Grand Prize:

The winning project with the highest number of votes will have their project showcased to the Google Cloud team!
